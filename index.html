Our Services

Our team specializes in utilizing reclaimed wood to renovate your home. We also provide exceptional carpentry skills and knowledge across the board. We are eager to assist you in any and all general renovations you have in mind!

Fine Carpentry

We provide skilled and artistic woodoworking service that creates beautiful and intricate finished products. Our expert team use traditional and modern techniques to build custom pieces and remodel your home. With our attention to detail and dedication to quality, we enhance the beauty and functionality of any space.

Reclaimed Wood

Our team partners with Real Antique Wood to provide the highest and finest quality materials to all of our projects. RAW recycles wood that would have otherwise gone to waste, and instead salvages to create anything you can dream of. Their mission of "giving antique wood a second lease on life" reflects our core values.

General Renovations

Beyond our specialization in reclaimed wood remodels, we are available to design and develop any and all renovations. Whether it be a home remodel or a business project, we are excited and ready to help produce the project of your dreams. We will consult with you to best ensure that your vision and our design harmoniously align.

About Our Team

Allow us to introduce you to our team of professional and motivated experts.

PAT O'SHEA

Pat is the owner, founder, and hands-on specialist of O'Shea Carpentry and Remodeling. Following a 15-year career as an NYC Union carpenter, Pat established the company in 2021 to bring you the specialized care and attention needed for all of your home projects.

JOHN O'SHEA

John is a recently retired New York City Union carpenter who brings 45 years of experience to the company. Having left his imprint on Manhattan's skyline, his focus now is on passing his knowledge to his son as they collaborate to remodel your home.

CHRIS GRASSO

Chris brings 10 years of professional carpentry experience to the company and serves as Pat's right-hand man. He brings reliability and braun to every project and can be trusted to get the job done the right way.
